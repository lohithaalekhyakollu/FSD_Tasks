RAM CHARAN

Ram Charan (born 27 March 1985) is an Indian actor, producer, and entrepreneur who works predominantly in Telugu cinema. One of the highest-paid actors in India, he has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2013. He is the recipient of several awards, including three Filmfare Awards and two Nandi Awards.

He is known for his intense acting and dancing skills and is popularly referred to as "Mega Power Star" in the media.

RAM CHARAN HAS STARRED IN BLOCKBUSTER FILMS SUCH AS MAGADHEERA (2009), RACHCHA (2012), NAYAK (2013), YEVADU (2014), DHRUVA (2016), RANGASTHALAM (2018), VINAYA VIDHEYA RAMA (2019), AND RRR (2022).
